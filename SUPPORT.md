# NOX Apothecary Support

For support with NOX Apothecary, please use this GitHub repository's issue tracker:

https://github.com/lanray07/NOX-Apothecary/issues

## What to Include

When requesting support, please include:

- Your device model
- Your iOS version
- The NOX Apothecary app version
- A short description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce the issue, if applicable

Please do not include sensitive health, medical, payment, or personal information in public issues.

## Wellness Disclaimer

NOX Apothecary provides wellness and lifestyle guidance only. It is not medical advice, psychological therapy, diagnosis, treatment, or a guarantee of outcomes. For medical, mental health, or safety concerns, consult a qualified professional.
