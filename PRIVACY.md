# NOX Apothecary Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 24 May 2026

NOX Apothecary is a wellness and lifestyle app for cinematic personal rituals, mood check-ins, breathwork, journaling prompts, herbal inspiration, sleep ceremonies, focus rituals, and ambient wellness experiences.

This policy explains how NOX Apothecary handles information in the current iOS release.

## Summary

NOX Apothecary does **not collect personal data from the app** in the current release.

The app is designed to work locally on your device with mock AI enabled by default. Mood check-ins, generated rituals, ritual preferences, saved rituals, personas, achievements, and insight history are stored locally on your device using Apple local persistence technologies.

## Information Stored Locally on Your Device

NOX Apothecary may store the following information locally on your device so the app can provide its core experience:

- Mood check-ins
- Stress, focus, energy, motivation, and sleep-quality entries
- Ritual preferences
- Generated rituals
- Saved or favourite rituals
- Herbal library preferences
- Ritual streaks
- Persona progress
- App settings
- Notification preferences

This information is not transmitted to NOX Apothecary servers in the current release.

## AI Features

NOX Apothecary includes AI ritual generation scaffolding. In the current release, mock AI is enabled by default and runs without sending your mood or ritual inputs to an external AI service.

If a future version enables a remote AI backend, this privacy policy and the App Store privacy details will be updated before that data practice is introduced.

## Subscriptions and Purchases

NOX Apothecary uses Apple StoreKit for subscriptions and in-app purchases. Purchase processing is handled by Apple. Apple may process purchase, billing, refund, and subscription status information under Apple's own privacy policy and App Store terms.

NOX Apothecary does not receive your full payment card details.

## Notifications

If you enable ritual reminders, NOX Apothecary may schedule local notifications on your device. Notification preferences are stored locally and can be changed in the app or in iOS Settings.

## Tracking, Advertising, and Analytics

NOX Apothecary does not use third-party advertising SDKs, does not track you across apps or websites, and does not collect analytics data in the current release.

## Wellness Disclaimer

NOX Apothecary provides wellness and lifestyle guidance only. It is not medical advice, psychological therapy, diagnosis, treatment, or a guarantee of outcomes. For medical, mental health, or safety concerns, consult a qualified professional.

## Children

NOX Apothecary is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device in the current release, you can delete local app data by using in-app data deletion controls where available or by deleting the app from your device.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

## Contact

For privacy questions about NOX Apothecary, use the support contact method provided on the app's App Store product page.
